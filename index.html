Data journalismmakes your newsroom smarter.

PANDA makes data journalism easier.

  • Information on deadline Your city’s data at your fingertips, available at the speed of breaking news.
  • Smarter, not harder Data stuck on a hard drive is sad data. PANDA helps reporters share, and tells you when someone else adds data you care about.
  • Institutional memory People are going to leave, but your data shouldn’t leave with them. Make it faster for new reporters to find stories in data.
  • Newsroom born & raised The Knight Foundation funded this software, built by news developers and free for you. PANDA is now sustained by the generous support of IRE.

PANDA is …

  • A private data library for your newsroom
  • A place for teams to share spreadsheets
  • A place to keep your FOIA requests
  • Your robot friend that emails you important news
  • A search engine for your names, phone numbers and more

… and in use at Chicago Tribune, The Dallas Morning News, and many other news organizations.
